gtk_distribute_natural_allocation

gint gtk_distribute_natural_allocation (gint extra_space,
                                        guint n_requested_sizes,
                                        GtkRequestedSize* sizes);

Distributes @extra_space to child @sizes by bringing smaller children up to natural size first. The remaining space will be added to the @minimum_size member of the GtkRequestedSize struct. If all sizes reach their natural size then the remaining space is returned. to @sizes.
